🤖 How Greekly works

  • Quizzes up to 20 questions — as many sessions a day as you want
  • Every question is generated by AI based on your profile — name, city, hobbies and goals — for maximum personalization
  • First 3 days — learning mode: Greekly covers all topics broadly
  • From day 4 — adaptive mode: weak topics appear more often, mastered topics less often
  • Rule explanation after every answer — so each mistake becomes a mini-lesson
  • Vocabulary memorization mode
  • Dialogues — practice everyday Greek in realistic situations
  • Writing training — short messages and emails in official style
