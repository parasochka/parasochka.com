About me

I’m Artem Parasochka, an SEO specialist and online marketer with 15 years of practice. I cover the full cycle of work: from site architecture and technical optimization to link strategy, analytics, and conversion growth.

My focus is on business metrics - visibility, quality organic traffic, CPL/CPA, and SEO’s contribution to revenue. I work at the intersection of technical and product-led SEO so that it strengthens marketing rather than competing with it.

Key tools

  • Crawlers: Screaming Frog, SEMrush, Netpeak Spider
  • Links and competitors: Ahrefs, MOZ, Netpeak Checker
  • Analytics: Google Analytics, Looker Studio, AppsFlyer
  • Webmaster panels: Google Search Console, Bing Webmaster Tools
  • Keywords: Keyword Planner, Key Collector, Keyword Tool
  • Ads and monetization: Google Ads, Google AdSense

If you’re looking for a systematic SEO process that speaks the language of business - not just rankings - I’d be glad to discuss your project.
